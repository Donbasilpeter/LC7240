WHAT DO WE DO WITH THE PULP?

The pulp from the Fresh-N-Fresh Juicer is more like paper. We typically put the pulp into compost for our gardens or worm bins. Some other ideas are crackers, using ginger pulp for tea, or dehydrating it into a powder.

Add some juicy fruit pulp to your pancake batter for a burst of fruity flavour and some added texture. The pulp will also provide some extra moisture, so you may want to reduce some of your other wet ingredients a little

Add interest to smoothies. Try adding some nutritious pulp to the blender when you make your next breakfast smoothie.
